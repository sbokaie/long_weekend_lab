20 Years. 5000+ Devices. 100% Mission Success.

Space

TiNi devices are regularly used by space agencies such as NASA, ESA, and JAXA. As a primary subcontractor to Lockheed Martin, Boeing and Orbital, TiNi actuators have been used on a multitude of spacecraft such as GPSIII, Pegasus and the Mars Surveyor.

Defense

TiNi Actuators have been used to release critical deployables aboard missile systems for the MOP and SM3 programs. Applications are numerous aboard missile systems.

Aviation

TiNi Actuators are used to deploy floatation systems aboard AW139 Helicopters. Similar systems may be used in conjunction with Search and Rescue equipment aboard military helicopters.

Underwater

Increasingly popular are TiNi’s release devices optimized for reliable underwater use. This was recently highlighted by the use of Pinpuller’s aboard MBARI’s AUV to help clean the oil spill in the Gulf of Mexico.

Our products

Frangibolt

TiNi Aerospace's Frangibolt family of actuators are designed for deployment of a wide variety of stowed appendages. The family of actuators are designed to release loads up to 20,000 lbf.

Pinpuller

The SMA actuated Pinpullers manufactured by TiNi Aerospace may be used to secure and release numerous deployables. TiNi's off the shelf Pinpullers range in force from 5 to 1000 lbs.

ERM

TiNi's Ejector Release Mechanism (ERM) is a uniquely simple and effective device which offers both high load holding capability and fast actuation time. The principal of operation is that a bolt is threaded into the coupler section of the Actuator and used to secure a spacecraft deployable.

Custom Actuators

To complement the Frangibolt and Pinpuller product lines, TiNi Aerospace has developed and qualified a host of other SMA actuators. TiNi Aerospace prides itself on the unique ability to quickly and efficiently engineer new actuator embodiments using our core SMA technology.
